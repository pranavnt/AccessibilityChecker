FAQ

What is AccessibilityChecker?

We are a team commited to web accessibility and inclusion! We aim to help developers check and improve the accessibility of their website.

Why should I use it?

Close to 13% of all people in the US have a disability, including people with low vision, people with a form of colorblindness. Making your website more INCLUSIVE will not only broaden the audience who can use your website, but it will make it easier for EVERYONE to use your website.

Why is accessibility important?

Web accessibility increases the potential audience of your website. Furthermore, valuing accessibility is correlated with significant improvements in the User Experience in your website.
